# Vendor Risk Dashboard  
APJ SecureCloud

This dashboard helps visualize vendor performance, highlights high-risk vendors, and gives leadership a quick view of supply chain risk.

Displays key fields:

### **B. Risk Color Coding**
Use conditional formatting:

- **Green:** Low Risk  
- **Yellow:** Medium Risk  
- **Red:** High Risk
